Customise your own platform

No two travel agencies are the same. That’s why Amadeus Selling Platform Connect has been designed so you can adapt it to your precise requirements. It’s a platform that’s as individual as your own travel business.

54% average time saving* when integrating external content

Amadeus Selling Platform Connect’s open ecosystem means you can work with us or any third-party developer to add whichever content or tool you need. You can even integrate the third-party content you want, directly into your booking flow, with automatic PNR synchronisation.

Stand out from the crowd

All agents working with the same version doesn’t mean they all need to have the same set of functionality – if there are features or functionalities that you don’t think they’ll need, you can simply turn them off. With in-built technological flexibility, you are free to devise your own strategy that will set you apart from your competitors.

Work the way you want to

You can design an Amadeus Selling Platform Connect package with a set-up of workflows to serve your business in the best way possible. Either customise a set of functionalities yourself or work with us to develop the specific customisation you are looking for. The system exists to support the way you want to work.

Work more efficiently

Deploy scripts throughout your whole network instantly, saving time and expense. Share your newly defined productivity rules with any other travel professional or office in your organisation. Your travel consultants will always have the same, latest version of the platform, which means that everyone can access the latest functionalities, including custom scripts. This standardisation of working processes, coupled with superior integration of productivity tools, ensures all your customers will enjoy the same excellent level of service.

*Average time savings based on Amadeus value calculations. If you wish to know the total time savings for your business, contact us.
