World’s most passionate travel agent competition

The world’s most passionate travel agent competition is a video competition for travel consultants. The objective of this competition is to find the world’s most passionate travel agents.

To participate, travel consultants must create and submit a video showing how they put their passion to work. Three winners from the top ten videos with the most votes on www.amadeus.com/share-your-passion will win a professionally-made video promoting their business and their passion through Amadeus channels.

Enjoy the competition and good luck!

Why should I participate?

To win one of the prizes of course! But surely, you also want to share your videos to showcase your passion for travel.

Who can participate?

All travel agents who are passionate about travel.

How can I participate?

Record a video showing how you put your passion for travel to work. As you can see, the possibilities are endless, so tap into your most creative self and share the results.

You can upload as many entries as you like.

Oh! And any entry you submit is entirely voluntary and gratuitous.

Keep your video short and snappy. Maximum length of the video is 5 minutes.

Winners will be announced on the Amadeus channels.

Additionally, winners will be notified through their e-mail address.
